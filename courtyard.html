Courtyard in London
A new built French style 18th century inspired home in the US. Lush planting, fruit trees, vegetables and a herb garden located close to the kitchen. Several entertainment areas and wonderful sculptures and art from the client’s collection. We added elegant classic outdoor garden furniture from Herve Baume, France to the client’s already existing furniture. It was a true pleasure to work with the client, who has a very good knowledge of horticulture and in addition a wonderful collection of garden ornaments.
